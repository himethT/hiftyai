What We Detect

Real-time AI detection for every safety scenario

Our custom AI models are trained specifically on your environment and industry — not generic off-the-shelf software.

PPE Detection

Hard hats, high-vis vests, safety gloves, goggles, masks, steel-toe boots — detects when any item is missing from any worker.

Restricted Zone Breach

Automatically flags any person entering a hazard zone, machine exclusion zone, or authorised-only area.

Fire & Smoke Detection

Early-stage fire and smoke detected through camera feeds before manual detection, triggering immediate site alerts.

Worker Fall Detection

AI identifies abnormal posture or fall events in real time, triggering emergency alert to supervisors immediately.

Crowd & Overcrowding Alerts

Detects dangerous crowding near machinery or in confined spaces, sending alerts before accidents occur.

Vehicle & Pedestrian Conflict

Monitors proximity between forklifts, vehicles and workers — alerts when unsafe distances are reached.
